About Me

My name is Joseph Greene. I am a developer interested in web development and UX research/design. I graduated from the University of California, Davis in June of 2021 with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Psychology, and a minor in Computer Science.

Web Dev and UX

Being a developer is always something I've considered as a possible career path, and I think that while my degree is in psychology rather than CS, many of the things I've learned about psychology are applicable to being a developer. Whether its communicating with clients in a way that's meaningful for both parties or designing products in a way that makes people want to use them, I think my psychology background gives me a good understanding of how to approach situations and problems.

What Im Learning

I began learning HTML, CSS, and javascript in late 2021 when I designed a custom character sheet for the Roll20 VTT, but only recently started learning how to use them for more standard web design purposes. I've also been learning python, and have been using PyQt to start working on a desktop application.
